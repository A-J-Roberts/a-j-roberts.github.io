Research Experience

  • 2024 - present
    PhD Research

    • Using MITgcm on high-performance computing systems
    • Focus on mechanisms and timescales of Antarctic changes propagating to global ocean circulation
  • 2023 - 2024
    Master's Research Project

    • Investigated Southern Ocean rotational forcing and latitude-dependent circulation responses to anthropogenic forcing
